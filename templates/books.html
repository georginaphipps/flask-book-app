Before We Say Goodbye - Toshikazu Kawaguchi

"Before We Say Goodbye" is a beautifully crafted novel that tugs at the heartstrings and reminds us of the preciousness of time and the importance of saying what needs to be said before it's too late. Kawaguchi's ability to create a magical, yet relatable world makes this book a compelling read for anyone who has ever wished for one more moment with a loved one. Whether you're a long-time fan of the series or a newcomer, this book offers a touching exploration of the human heart and the timeless bonds that connect us all. Grab a cup of coffee, settle into your favorite reading nook, and let "Before We Say Goodbye" take you on a journey through time and emotion.


Welcome To The Hyunam-Dong Bookshop - Hwang Bo-Reum

"Welcome to the Hyunam-Dong Bookshop" is a charming and uplifting novel that celebrates the magic of books and the power of human connection. Hwang Bo-Reum crafts a narrative that is both touching and inspiring, making it a perfect read for anyone who has ever found solace in a book or a sense of belonging in a special place. Whether you're a bibliophile or simply someone in search of a heartwarming story, this book promises to be a delightful journey that will leave you feeling enriched and inspired. So, step into the Hyunam-Dong Bookshop, and let yourself be swept away by its magic.


Convenience Store Woman - Sayaka Murata

"Convenience Store Woman" is a compelling and unconventional novel that challenges readers to rethink societal norms and the concept of fulfillment. Sayaka Murata crafts a story that is both engaging and thought-provoking, with a protagonist whose unconventional life invites empathy and reflection. For readers interested in character-driven stories that question societal expectations and explore the complexities of identity, "Convenience Store Woman" offers a refreshing and insightful perspective. It's a short yet impactful read that leaves a lasting impression, encouraging readers to embrace their individuality and find their own path to happiness. Whether you're intrigued by contemporary Japanese literature or seeking a novel that challenges conventional thinking, "Convenience Store Woman" is a must-read. Step into Keiko's world, and you might just find yourself questioning what it means to live a “normal” life.
